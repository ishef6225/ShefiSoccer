Training

a game played by two teams of eleven players with a round ball that may not be touched with the hands or arms during play except by the goalkeepers. The object of the game is to score goals by kicking or heading the ball into the opponents' goal.


Practicing

Training usuaclly occurs when you practice with your team. You run different drills provided by your coach to make yourself better.

Working out is also a key form of training in soccer. Staying at your top physical condition is important for this sport. Drills and games require you to have lots of stamina and strength.


Stength Development

.

Good soccer teams will not only develop your mental and tactical skills on the field but your strentgh as well. Soccer is a very physical sport and it requires you not to only be better then your oponent but stronger as well.
